Elektra's Portfolio

Elektra Caballes, a 21 yr old, Bachelors in Interior Architecture Student. Passionate about creating spaces that not only meet functional needs but also inspire and enhance the human experience.

My design philosophy centers around the seamless integration of form, function, and environment. I believe that architecture has the power to shape communities and influence the way we live, work, and interact with our surroundings. Each project I undertake is approached with a commitment to excellence, creativity, and attention to detail.

In this portfolio, you will find a selection of my work, ranging from residential and commercial projects to urban planning and landscape architecture. Each project showcases my ability to adapt to different styles and requirements while maintaining a consistent standard of quality and innovation.
